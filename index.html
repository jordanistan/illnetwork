  • Our Services

    • Vulnerability Assessments

      We identify and assess vulnerabilities in your systems, applications, and network infrastructure. Our team uses advanced scanning tools to detect security gaps and provide actionable steps to mitigate risks.

      Tools: Nessus, Qualys, OpenVAS, Burp Suite

    • Penetration Testing

      Simulating real-world cyberattacks to assess how vulnerable your systems are. We perform both external and internal pen tests to identify weaknesses that could be exploited by attackers.

      Tools: Metasploit, Burp Suite, Nmap, OWASP ZAP, Kali Linux, Cobalt Strike

    • Compliance Audits

      We guide your organization through regulatory compliance processes such as PCI DSS, HIPAA, GDPR, SOC 2, and more. Our audits will assess your security posture and offer clear guidance on achieving and maintaining compliance.

      Tools: Tenable.io, Qualys, Nessus, Drata, Vanta

    • Incident Response and Forensics

      In the event of a security breach, our Incident Response team is ready to act fast. We investigate the breach, mitigate further damage, and provide forensic analysis to understand the cause and prevent future incidents.

      Tools: Splunk, CrowdStrike, Wireshark, Mandiant, SIFT Workstation, EnCase

    • Cloud Security

      We offer security solutions for cloud platforms like AWS, Google Cloud, and Azure. From setting up secure cloud environments to automating security controls, our services ensure your cloud infrastructure is fully protected.

      Tools: AWS GuardDuty, Azure Security Center, Prisma Cloud, Terraform, AWS IAM

    • Security Awareness Training

      Human error is often the weakest link in security. We provide customized security training to educate your staff on cyber threats, safe online behavior, phishing prevention, and secure password practices.

      Tools: KnowBe4, PhishMe, Infosec IQ, CyberHoot

    • SIEM Implementation

      We design and implement SIEM systems that provide centralized monitoring, real-time analysis, and alerts for suspicious activity across your infrastructure.

      Tools: Splunk, IBM QRadar, LogRhythm, AlienVault, Elastic SIEM

    • Network Security

      Our network security services include firewall configurations, intrusion detection/prevention, and secure VPN setup. We assess your network's security posture and protect against potential attacks.

      Tools: Cisco ASA, Palo Alto Networks, Fortinet, pfSense, Snort

    • Identity and Access Management (IAM)

      We implement IAM systems that ensure only authorized users can access sensitive information. Our solutions range from role-based access control to multi-factor authentication and zero trust architectures.

      Tools: Okta, Ping Identity, Microsoft Azure AD, Duo Security, AWS IAM

    • Security Architecture Review

      Our security architecture review services analyze your current infrastructure to ensure it’s robust and resilient against cyber threats. We help design secure system and application architectures that follow best practices.

      Tools: AWS Trusted Advisor, Microsoft Secure Score, Splunk, IBM Security AppScan

    • Application Security

      We secure your software applications by performing thorough code reviews, penetration testing, and dynamic application security testing. We ensure your applications are hardened against attacks during development.

      Tools: Veracode, Checkmarx, Snyk, SonarQube, Fortify

    • Red Team/Blue Team Exercises

      We conduct red and blue team exercises to simulate attacks and improve your organization's defenses. Our Red Team identifies vulnerabilities through offensive testing, while our Blue Team builds and improves defensive strategies.

      Tools: Kali Linux, Cobalt Strike (Red Team), Splunk, IBM QRadar (Blue Team)

    • Data Encryption Services

      We provide encryption solutions for data at rest and in transit, ensuring that your sensitive information remains secure. Our experts will help you implement encryption protocols to meet both regulatory and business needs.

      Tools: OpenSSL, BitLocker, AWS KMS, HashiCorp Vault, GnuPG

    • Endpoint Security Management

      We secure all endpoints (laptops, smartphones, tablets) from malware, unauthorized access, and data breaches. Our endpoint protection ensures that your devices remain secure, no matter where they are.

      Tools: CrowdStrike Falcon, Carbon Black, Microsoft Defender ATP, SentinelOne, McAfee ePO

    • Third-Party Vendor Risk Assessments

      We evaluate the security postures of your third-party vendors to ensure they align with your organization’s security standards. Our assessments mitigate risks that may arise from partnerships and outsourcing.

      Tools: BitSight, SecurityScorecard, Prevalent, Panorays
